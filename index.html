📜Rules📜


Each turn🎮, a player repeatedly rolls a dice🎲 until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "hold":

👉If the player rolls🔄️ a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.

👉If the player rolls🔄️ any other number, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues.

👉If a player chooses to "hold🐖", their turn total is added to their score, and it becomes the next player's turn.

🤩The first player to meet target score (50 / 100) or more points wins🏆.
